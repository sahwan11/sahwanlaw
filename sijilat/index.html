Frequently Asked Questions

How long does company registration take? +

Typically 3-7 business days from submission of complete documents. Sijili (sole proprietorship) can be faster at 2-3 days. Complex structures or regulated activities may take longer.

How much does it cost? +

Our service fee starts from 250 BHD for Sijili and 400 BHD for WLL. Government fees (CR issuance, municipality, LMRA) are additional and vary by activity type. We provide a full cost breakdown before you commit.

Can foreigners own 100% of a company? +

Yes. Bahrain allows 100% foreign ownership for most commercial activities. Some regulated sectors (e.g., real estate, media) may require a Bahraini partner or special licensing from the relevant authority.

What if my application is rejected? +

We review the rejection reason, make corrections, and resubmit at no additional charge. Most rejections are due to commercial name conflicts or incomplete documents, which we proactively check before submission.

Do I need to be physically present in Bahrain? +

Not necessarily. With a notarized Power of Attorney, we can handle the entire registration remotely. You may need to visit for e-Key activation or bank account opening at certain stages.

What is the refund policy? +

If we are unable to complete your registration due to reasons within our control, we offer a full refund of our service fee. Government fees paid to Sijilat are non-refundable as per MOIC policy.
