Managing Partner

Abdulla Sahwan

Leading Sahwan Law's strategic direction and continuing his father's legacy of legal excellence in Bahrain since 2009.

Abdulla Sahwan serves as the Managing Partner of Salman A. Sahwan - Attorneys & Legal Consultants (Sahwan Law), one of Bahrain's most established law firms. Following the passing of the firm's founder, Salman Abdulla Sahwan (1948-2009), Abdulla assumed leadership of the practice his father built from humble beginnings in Manama's traditional souq in 1975.

Under his leadership, Sahwan Law has modernized its operations while preserving the client-centered values that defined its founding. The firm has embraced cloud-based infrastructure, expanded its GCC network through strategic partnerships with Abdul Hameed Alsarraf Law Firm in Kuwait and The Firm in Dubai, and became one of the first two private notarization services to obtain the official Bahrain notary seal.

Abdulla brings a strategic, forward-thinking approach to legal practice, combining deep expertise in Bahraini law with an understanding of international business dynamics. He personally oversees complex matters spanning corporate transactions, real estate development, banking and finance, and high-stakes litigation.

The firm's client roster reflects this commitment to excellence, serving prestigious organizations including Amwaj Islands, Bahrain Duty Free, Art Rotana Hotels, Al Zain Jewellery, and many of Bahrain's most prominent business families.
