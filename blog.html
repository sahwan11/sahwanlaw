Commercial Law May 31, 2025

Bolstering Commercial Integrity: Major Overhaul of Cheque Regulations in Bahrain's Commercial Law (Law No. 23 of 2025)

Bahrain's Law No. (23) of 2025 significantly overhauls check regulations within its Commercial Law to bolster financial stability and commercial integrity. Key changes include mandating partial payment of checks when full funds are unavailable, making checks with insufficient funds directly enforceable executive instruments.
